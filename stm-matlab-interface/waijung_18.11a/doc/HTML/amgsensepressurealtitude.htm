aMG Sense - Pressure / Altitude

 

Features

1.Based on Freescale MPL3115A2
2.Fully Compensated internally
3.Direct Reading, Compensated
•Pressure: 20-bit measurement (Pascals)
•Altitude: 20-bit measurement (meters)
•Temperature: 12-bit measurement (degrees Celsius)
4.Programmable Events
5.Autonomous Data Acquisition
6.Resolution down to 1 ft. / 30 cm
7.32 Sample FIFO
8.Ability to log data up to 12 days using the FIFO
9.1 second to 9 hour data acquisition rate
10.I2C digital output interface (operates up to 400 kHz)
11.Power Supply: 1.95V to 3.6V, internally regulated by LDO
12.1.6V to 3.6V Digital Interface Supply Voltage

Applications

1.High Accuracy Altimetry
2.Smartphones/Tablets
3.Personal Electronics Altimetry
4.GPS Dead Reckoning
5.GPS Enhancement for Emergency Services
6.Map Assist, Navigation
7.Weather Station Equipment
